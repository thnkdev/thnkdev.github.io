Snapped
The one and only way to have Snapchat on your Mac.
Snapped is the ultimate Snapchat client.

You can send and receive "snaps" just like you would on the iPhone client, but Snapped actually offers features that the iOS client doesn't.

You can mark "snaps" that you send and quickly regret as read!

You have the option to never mark "snaps" that were sent to you as read so you can keep them forever!
Snapped was built with care for Mac OS X 10.9 Mavericks and will be available on the App Store soon.
